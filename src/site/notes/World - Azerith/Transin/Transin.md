	 A land of icy coldness where it seems to be winter year-round. A snowglobe-like setting where everything seems to be pristine at all times. The magical blessing of Glacious allows for this 'winter wonderland' of a faction. 

## Information

**Political System:** Transin is the only traditional monarchy in Azerith. The faction is ruled by a queen or king known as the "King/Queen of Frost." The 'frost hood' is passed down to the leader's eldest child when the leader dies or abdicates their throne. It is believed that the bloodline of the throne descends from [[World - Azerith/The Gods#The Pantheon\|Glacious]] themself. The current leader is Queen 

**Border:** The government of Transin has control over all of the land that is regularly icy and cold. 

**Capital:** [[World - Azerith/Transin/Quish\|Quish]]

**Climate:** Due to the blessing of Glacious, this faction has a climate not unlike the climate of the poles on Earth. It is cold all year long, but it gets even colder in the winter. In the summer, the days are long (there can be up to 27 hours of sunlight in the summer), and in the winter, the days are short. 

**Defining Feature:** Cold

## Geography 

[[World - Azerith/Azerith#Geography\|The Reani River]] is a large river that runs through Transin. It is frozen most of the year, although there are times in the summer when parts are liquid. When frozen, the snow is cleared and the river is used as a main road to [[World - Azerith/Transin/Quish\|Quish]].

In addition to this, there are two small forests on the outskirts of Transin. They are called the Alderwyn Glade and the Snowy Grove. These are very typical snowy forests with fir and pine trees often covered in a layer of soft snow. 

Alderwyn Glade has many small ruins of old towns and temples.
