	A small 'Roman' style city with small sand coloured homes and a coliseum. 

## Information

**Population:** Small City

Although in the grand scheme of things, Animos is a small city, it is quite large for [[World - Azerith/Groustia/Groustia\|Groustia]]. Due to the harsh conditions of Groustia, any settlements are quite small, and this makes Animos a big deal. 

In the centre of Animos, there is a large coliseum, and other small coliseums are scattered throughout the city. The small coliseums are used primarily for sporting events or special occasions like weddings. 

The coliseum battles to determine the leader of Groustia take place once every three years in the central coliseum of Animo. Otherwise, it is used for a daily market where you can buy just about anything you can imagine.
