	 A nation of heat and desert where the sun beats down any any foolish enough to venture unprepared. Full of large scorpians and snakes, Groustia is a dangerous land to traverse. 

## Information

**Political System:** The leader of Groustia is whoever is the physically strongest. Every three years, a large gladiator fight takes place, with the winner becoming the leader for the next three years. 

**Border:** The consensus is that Groustia encompasses the entire northern desert and nothing else. There is some dispute over whether or not this includes the edge of the Lavastian forest. 

**Capital:** [[World - Azerith/Groustia/Animos\|Animos]]

**Climate:** Hot and dry. 

**Defining Feature:** [[World - Azerith/Azerith#Desert & Tundra\|Desert]]

## Geography 

Groustia has a hot desert climate with forests of dry trees surrounded by seemingly endless sand in every direction. Although it does rain in the winter, it is dry for months on end, which severely limits the population of Groustia. This is the reason for Groustia being made of many small villages compared to the large cities elsewhere. 

There are a few key geographical features in Groustia. There are two large forests, the Shadowed Sky and the Burning Field. 

The Shadowed Sky is named after the fact that the trees there are tall and jet black, often confusing as they block out the sun. Locals warn of times when all you can see is darkness once entering the forest. The Shadowed Sky is home to many dangerous monsters that roam the darkness. 

The Burning Field is almost the complete opposite. Due to the dryness of the area, the forest is almost perpetually burning. Smoke can be seen from miles away from the forest, making it a key landmark for travelling merchants and nomads alike. 

In addition to the forests, there are three mountains in Groustia. These are Pine Tower (near [[World - Azerith/Lavastia/Lavastia\|Lavastia]]), Poison Tooth (near [[World - Azerith/Shallow Hills/Shallow Hills\|Shallow Hills]]), and Flaming Spear (near Burning Field). These mountains are hard to climb and dangerous.
