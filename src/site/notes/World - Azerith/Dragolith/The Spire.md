	A tower taller than the highest trees in Lavastia, The Spire is the capital of Dragolith. 

## Information

**Population**: Medium-sized City

The spire is a huge circular building that stretches high into the sky while also having a portion underground. Around the spire is a more typical city (which is also considered to be a part of The Spire).

Life in The Spire can vary a lot from person to person. Some people live inside the spire while others live in the city surrounding it.  Most wealthy or politically important people live on the upper floors of The Spire, while poorer people live near the bottom or underground. 

Just like all other places in Dragolith, domesticated dragons are used for a variety of tasks. From transport, construction, war, or even as pets, dragons are all around the city
