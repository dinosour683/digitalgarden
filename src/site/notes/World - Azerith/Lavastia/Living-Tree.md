	As elves swing from trees and dwarves build homes on the ground, the bustling city of Living-Tree is the heart of Lavastia. 

## Information

**Population:** Large City

Living-Tree is the bustling capital of [[World - Azerith/Lavastia/Lavastia\|Lavastia]]. There are a variety of home styles in Living-Tree. Some people live in tree houses with bridges of rope and vine connecting them, others live in regular mossy homes on the ground, and some live underground in the roots of the giant Lavastian trees. 

Temples scatter the ground of Living-Tree, but in the city's centre, there is a large temple-like building made of stone, moss, and glass. This building is known as "The Atrium." The Atrium has many purposes, from a city hall, a temple to Graer, to a weekly market. However, the main purpose of The Atrium is to serve as a meeting place for [[World - Azerith/Lavastia/Lavastia#Information\|The Council]]. This is where The Council holds their meetings to vote on laws and polices for Lavastia.
