A small, densely packed village in the south of [[World - Azerith/Lavastia/Lavastia\|Lavastia]], around a 5-day journey south-west of [[World - Azerith/Lavastia/Living-Tree\|Living-Tree]]. 

The people of Maenin are very proud of being a part of Lavastia, and regularly have rituals to honour [[World - Azerith/The Gods#The Pantheon\|Graer]], the god of plants and the forest. 

# Features

There are a few interesting locations in Maenin. 

## The Well

In the centre of the town, there is a beautiful well. People often flick coins in for good luck, or hoping for a wish to come true. 

This well is the heart of the village, with many special occasions (weddings, anniversaries, proposals, etc.) taking place here. 

## The Temple

The village is too small to have a temple dedicated to each god, so instead, they have one large temple that is used for everyone. 

The main god in Maenin is Graer, but other gods are worshipped too. 

## The Pigs Stye Inn

The one inn and tavern in town, the pig stye, is a bustling place for social activities. 

Bards play music as people meet up with friends and family for a drink after work. 

## The Market

The market is the main way that people make purchases in town. Your groceries, adventuring tools, and even weapons can be bought here. 

Between the regular tents that are there every day and the travelling merchants that stop in every once in a while, anything you could want to buy can be bought here if you have the cash.
