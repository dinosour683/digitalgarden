	A land of ever-expanding and seemingly never-ending forests in every direction. Towering trees reach upwards, scraping the sky. In the eastern corner of Lavastia is one large forested area where people live in trees, underground, or even on top of large trees. 

## Information

**Political System:** Lavastia is run by a parliament-like group known as 'the council.' Once you are elected onto the council, you are a member until you die or are deemed unable to do your job by 2/3 council members. The vast majority of council members rule until death, meaning that due to the high elven population, some council members rule for centuries. 

**Border:** Lavastia rules over the entire green forest in the southeast of [[World - Azerith/Azerith\|Azerith]]. 

**Capital:** [[World - Azerith/Lavastia/Living-Tree\|Living-Tree]]

**Climate:** Very wet but not overly humid or warm. In the winter, it may get rainy or even snowy. 

**Defining Feature:** Forest

## Geography

There are not many large or interesting geographical features in Lavastia. The entire faction is one large forest with no major clearings. 

## The Infection

One issue in Lavastia is "The Infection." Every once in a while, a strange disease seems to appear out of nowhere and slowly spread through Lavastia. This disease corrupts living things, turning them evil, almost rabid. Animals attack for no reason, plants die and bear poisonous fruit. There is no known cure; however, it is quite easy to contain simply by quarantining and then killing anything infected. 

The first recorded case of The Infection was around 500 years ago, but thankfully, no one has reported any infection in around 50 years.
