	 Tall mounds of dirt and rock are the heart of this faction. In the northern part of Azerith, large hills almost resemble mountain-sized molehills; these mounds define Shallow Hills. 

## Information

**Political System:** A parliamentary system slightly different from South Africa, Canada, or the UK. Every four years, a nationwide vote is held. The ratios of votes for different parties are reflected in the ratio of members in the parliament. 

**Border:** Shallow Hills is essentially made up of all the leftover land. The border goes from [[World - Azerith/Azerith#The Gaping Maw\|The Gaping Maw]] down to the edge of [[World - Azerith/Transin/Transin\|Transin]], all the way across the edge of the [[World - Azerith/Lavastia/Lavastia\|Lavastian Forest]] and then northwest across the edge of the [[World - Azerith/Groustia/Groustia\|Groustian Desert]]. 

**Capital:** [[World - Azerith/Shallow Hills/Hannoia\|Hannoia]]

**Climate:** Similar to [[World - Azerith/Dragolith/Dragolith\|Dragolith]] with a generally warm climate. In the winter, it is rainy and cloudy, but snow is rare. 

**Defining Feature:** Mounds/Hills

## Geography 

Many large hills spot the horizon of Shallow Hills. These large, mountain-sized dirt mounds are where most people in the area find their homes. Homes are built on top, under, or even inside these hills (although each hill typically only has one kind of home).

No one is exactly sure how the mounds got here. Some say it was giant moles, others believe it was the gods fighting with dirt cannonballs. The exact truth is still unknown, but scientists and magicians are trying to discover more every day. 

In addition to this, there is one hill that collapsed long ago, leaving nothing but boulders and rubble. This collapse killed many in the area, making many believe that it is haunted. This site is known as The Crumbled Hill.
