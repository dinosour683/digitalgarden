	The capital of Shallow Hills is a large city built atop the tallest hill in the faction. 

## Information

**Population:** Large City

The city of Hannoia is the largest in [[World - Azerith/Shallow Hills/Shallow Hills\|Shallow Hills]]. It is built on top of the largest hill (often referred to as "the Hannoia Hill" or "the hill of Hannoia"). Most of the buildings here are made on top of the hill, but some are built on the side of the mountain (almost like hobbit holes). 

At the very top of the mound is a large castle-like structure known as The Keystone. This building is the root of all culture, history, and politics in Shallow Hills. Not only is it a museum going over the entire history of Shallow Hills, but it is also where the Shallow Hills Parliament meets every month.
