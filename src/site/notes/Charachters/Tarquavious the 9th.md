## Backstory:

Born and raised in [[World - Azerith/Dragolith/The Spire\|The Spire]] (the capital city of [[World - Azerith/Dragolith/Dragolith\|Dragolith]], Tarquavious the 9th spent his early days running from his house in the middle of the Spire to the Church of [[World - Azerith/The Gods#The Pantheon\|Stella]], where his father, Tarquavious the 8th, worked. 

As the head of the local church, his father worked long, hard days, and Tarq often spent time at church to help his father. His playground was the church's library, his toys were sigils of his god, and his free time was spent learning as much as he could about [[World - Azerith/The Gods#the pantheon\|Stella]]. 

As he grew up, he fell more and more in love with the god his father dedicated his life to. Then his mother died. One day, his mother was gone, no wounds, no illness, just dead. His father became obsessed with solving the mystery of his wife's death, but to no avail.

His father became so filled with grief that he had to step down from being the head priest at the church. This filled Tarq with dedication, and he decided to become a priest of Stella to eventually take his father's old job. He spent hours and hours studying and practising magic in order to eventually become the new head priest of The Spire's church of Stella. 

When he came of age, he enrolled in The Stellarian Academy to train to become a Seraph of Stella. There, he met [[Charachters/Theseus Butler\|Theseus Butler]], a human man training to become a warrior of Stella, and they quickly became good friends. After years of studying and practice, he finally finished the first half of his training. The only step left was to prove his worth by going out adventuring and helping people in the name of Stella. 

Theseus was assigned to be his Guardian, and Theseus insisted on beginning their quest in a small Lavastian village called [[World - Azerith/Lavastia/Maenin\|Maenin]]. Although Theseus would not explain why, Tarq decided to trust his friend and make the journey.
